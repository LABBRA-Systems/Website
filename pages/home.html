Common questions

Do you store faces or license plates?
No. We do not store faces or license plates, and we do not attempt to identify individuals or vehicles.
What is retained after a verified crash?
A short anonymized clip with a timestamp may be retained for safety, insurance, and reconstruction.
Do you support right to be forgotten requests?
Because we do not store identity-based data, individuals are not directly identifiable in our system.
Can you provide raw footage to third parties?
No. We cannot provide raw footage because we do not store raw footage.
