Safety Without Surveillance

LABBRA is a roadside safety system focused on detecting crashes, hazards, and roadway risks — not on tracking people or vehicles.

All RGB video is anonymized on‑device before it ever leaves a unit. No faces. No license plates. No identity tracking.

Only when a verified crash is detected does the device temporarily retain a short encrypted clip for public safety investigations. This forensic mode cannot be activated manually and footage is auto‑deleted unless formally requested by authorities.
