Who's Building LABBRA

What started as a university research project is becoming a real company, built on a simple idea: roads should be safer for everyone, without high costs or complicated infrastructure.

Field data collection with Gen 2 hardware

Benjamin Mingst

Founder & CEO

Benjamin is a software engineer who designs LABBRA's system architecture and has led hardware development through three generations. He was inspired to start this project after rollerblading over 1,500 miles across Florida and experiencing too many close calls with vehicles firsthand.

The name LABBRA honors six pedestrians who were killed near the University of Central Florida campus. Benjamin walked past several of their memorials every day, and that experience drove him to turn a research idea into something real.
