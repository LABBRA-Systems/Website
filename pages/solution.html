Privacy & Safety

Not an ALPR

We do not identify people or vehicles, and we do not track anyone.

Privacy by architecture

Raw data is processed in RAM and never stored or transmitted in raw form. Faces and plates are anonymized on device.

Anonymized retention

Normal operation keeps anonymized data only. After a verified crash, a short anonymized clip with timestamp may be retained.

Lawful requests

Retained data is anonymized and encrypted and not suitable for movement tracking. We do not store raw footage.
