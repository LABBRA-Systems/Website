Advanced Impact Detection System for Pedestrian Safety

About the LABBRA System

The LABBRA System is an innovative, advanced impact detection technology designed to protect pedestrians by reducing response times in emergency situations.

With a vision detection system powered by edge computing devices and wide-angle cameras, it helps emergency responders act quickly and efficiently, potentially saving lives. Named after six of the UCF students (London, Alexandria, Brooke, Brandon, Reuel, Austin) who tragically lost their lives while walking around campus, the LABBRA System honors their memory by improving pedestrian safety through cutting-edge technology.

The Problem

Understanding the Scale of Pedestrian Deaths

A Growing Crisis

On average in 2024, two pedestrians died every day on Florida's streets, totaling 686 deaths. This number continues to rise both statewide and nationally.

We are reminded of these tragedies every day when we cross the road next to Brooke Dawkins' memorial. As students, we have witnessed many close calls near campus. While some proactive measures exist, current solutions are costly, hard to scale, and preventative. To truly save lives, we need a reactive system that responds in real-time.

Florida Crash Dashboard - Showing 686 pedestrian fatalities in 2024, with 10,480 pedestrian crashes reported (FLHSMV).

Pedestrian Deaths in the UCF Area - Every blue dot represents a pedestrian death near campus and surrounding areas, between 2008 and 2022 (Smart Growth America).

We Need a Solution

The time for action is now. Every day we wait, more lives are lost.

The LABBRA System Solution

How LABBRA Works

Advanced technology for real-time pedestrian safety

The LABBRA System uses cutting-edge computer vision and edge computing to detect pedestrian accidents in real-time. Our system is designed to be cost-effective, scalable, and highly reliable, providing immediate alerts to emergency services when accidents occur.

Technical Specifications

Built with precision and reliability in mind

AI-Powered Detection

Machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of hours of traffic data to accurately identify pedestrian accidents.

Instant Alerts

Automatic 911 dispatch with GPS coordinates and accident details, reducing response time by up to 40%.

Weather Resistant

IP67 rated housing ensures reliable operation in all weather conditions, 24/7/365.
